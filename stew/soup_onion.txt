--------------------------------------------------
Yield: 17 liters
--------------------------------------------------

Ingredients

Oil, olive: 1 liter
Onions, Red, Yellow, White: 7 kilograms (30 medium counts)
Garlic, Minced: 350 grams (about 375 milliliters)
Broth, Vegetable: 10 liters
Soy Sauce: 250-350 milliliters (to taste)
Thyme, Dry: 50 grams (about 200 milliliters)
Bay Leaf: 25 count
Pepper, Black: 50 grams (about 75 milliliters)
Vinegar, Balsamic: 100 milliliters
Wine, Red Merlot: 750 milliliters

Procedure

Remove skins and tough parts from onions.
Slice onions lengthwise into quarters.
Slice onions into thin strips, 0.5-1 centimeters thick.
Use 6-7 kilograms of onion slices.

Heat oil in a pot at medium-high heat.
Combine onions with oil.
Cook onions in oil until they are tender, about 30 minutes.
Combine garlic with onions in oil.
Cook garlic and onions together in oil until onions are thoroughly tender, about 30 minutes.

Combine vegetable broth with other ingredients.
Combine soy sauce (to taste) with other ingredients.
Combine thyme, bay leaves, and black pepper with other ingredients.
Combine vinegar and wine with other ingredients.
Simmer soup together for 30 minutes.
