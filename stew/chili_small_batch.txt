--------------------------------------------------
Chili, Small Batch
--------------------------------------------------
--------------------------------------------------
--------------------------------------------------
Initial Preparation: 18 October 2023
--------------------------------------------------
--------------------------------------------------
--------------------------------------------------

Yield: 5 liters
Preparation time: 1.5 hours
   - Soak the beans for about 12 hours.
   - Cook the beans for about 2-3 hours on stove top if not in a pressure cooker.

Ingredients
Beans, dry: 1.0 kilogram
   - Black beans are a good choice.
   - Alternatively consider a combination of multiple types of beans.
   - Black, kidney, pinto, white, garbanzo, etc.

Oil, olive: 0.25 cup
Onions, chopped: 2 medium to large counts
Bell peppers, chopped: 2 large counts
Peppers: whatever you like
Vegetables: whatever you like
Tomatoes, chopped with juice or from can: 2-3 cups
Garlic, minced: 1 tablespoon
Cumin: 1-2 tablespoons
Coriander (optional): 1 tablespoon
Red pepper flakes: 1 teaspoon
Broth: enough to bring total volume to 5 liters
Salt: 1 tablespoon
Milk, whole (optional): 2 cups
Corn meal: 0.5 cup

Procedure

Cull beans.
Wash beans.
Soak beans in cool water for 10-20 hours.
Wash beans.
Cook beans until tender.

Cook onion, pepper, and other raw vegetables in oil.
Add all spices and seasonings.
Add tomatoes.
Add beans.
Add corn meal.
Add milk.
If necessary, add enough water to cover all solid ingredients and bring total volume to 5 liters.
Stew combination for 30 minutes.
