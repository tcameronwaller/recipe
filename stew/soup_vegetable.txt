Yield: 20 liters
Preparation time: 7 hours
--------------------------------------------------

Basic Ingredients

oil, olive: 1 liter
onions, chopped: 3 kilograms (15 medium counts)
garlic, minced: 350 grams (about 375 milliliters)
tomatoes, stewed with juice: 5 liters
potatoes, chopped: 4 kilograms
carrots, chopped: 2 kilograms

thyme, dry: 10 grams (about 30 milliliters)
basil, dry: 10 grams (about 30 milliliters)
oregano, dry: 10 grams (about 30 milliliters)
salt: _____ (about 15 milliliters)

Custom Ingredients
Consider including vegetables that are readily available, such as from a harvest or from good deals at a farmer's market or grocery store.

peppers, bell or other medium to mild, chopped: about 15 large counts
peas: 1 kilogram
kale: a lot
celery
squash

The soup's taste fits well with a little spice from peppers.
If not using peppers with spice, then consider adding some dry cayenne pepper.

Procedure

Prepare all vegetables.

Heat oil in a pot at medium-high heat.
Combine onions with oil.
Cook onions in oil until they are tender, about 30 minutes.
Combine garlic in oil.
Combine peppers in oil.
Cook onions, garlic, and peppers until all are tender.
Combine all vegetables and spices.
Add about 2 liters of water to bring total volume to 20 liters.

Simmer soup for about 3 hours until all vegetables are tender.
