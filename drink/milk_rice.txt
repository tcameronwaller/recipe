----------

----------
Yield: 15 liters
----------

----------
Ingredients
----------

Rice
Rice, brown: 2 kilogram (about 10 cup)
Water: 7 liters
Rice, brown, cooked: 30 cups

----------
Procedure
----------

Wash rice.
Cook rice in water at gentle boil until rice soaks up all water and is tender.

Slurry
Suspend rice in water.
Blend rice thoroughly in water.
Use about 12 cups rice for each liter of water.
Strain slurry through a strainer with a cloth.
