Flax Milk
--------------------------------------------------
--------------------------------------------------
--------------------------------------------------

----------
----------
----------
References

https://www.healthfulpursuit.com/2012/04/homemade-flax-milk/
----------
----------
----------

----------
Yield: 15 liters
----------

----------
Ingredients
----------

Flax seed: 4 cups
Water: 15 liters

----------
Procedure
----------

Blend 0.25 cups of flax seeds in each liter of water.
Strain slurry through a strainer with a cloth.

----------
Storage
----------

Store slurry with refrigeration for up to 3 weeks.
