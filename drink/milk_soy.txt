----------
Yield: 15 liters
----------

----------
Ingredients
----------

Soy
Soy beans, dry: 1.5 kilogram (about 8 cup)

Slurry
Water: 15 liters
Salt: 1 teaspoon (to taste)

Optional (per liter)
Vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon
Or
Nutmeg: 0.25 teaspoon
Or
Cocoa powder: 1 tablespoon

----------
Procedure
----------

Soy
Cull beans.
Wash beans.
Soak beans in cool water for 10-20 hours.
Wash beans.
Drain water from beans.

Slurry
Suspend beans in water.
Blend beans thoroughly in water.
Use about 1.5 cup beans for each liter of water.
Strain slurry through a strainer with a cloth.
Heat the slurry to a boil.
Simmer slurry for 20 minutes.
Cool slurry.
Add salt and other seasonings.

Optionally combine soy milk with flax milk, oat milk, or rice milk.
Prepare the combination together for stronger, thicker milk.

----------
Storage
----------

Store slurry with refrigeration for up to 3 weeks.
