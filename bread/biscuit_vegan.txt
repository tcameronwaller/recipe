Ingredients

Almond milk: 750 milliliters (about 3 cups)
Lemon juice: 50 milliliters (about 3 tablespoons)

Flour, white: 900 grams (about 5-6 cups)
Baking powder: 50 grams (about 4 tablespoons)
Baking soda: 10 grams (about 1.5 teaspoons)
Salt: 25 grams (about 3 teaspoons)

Oil, coconut: 200 grams (about 0.75 cup)

Procedure

Combine almond milk and lemon juice and let set for about 5 minutes.

Combine dry ingredients.
Cut coconut oil into dry ingredients, leaving pieces of about the size of peas.
It can help to chill the coconut oil before cutting it in.

Combine liquid ingredients with dry ingredients.
Mix dough just enough to combine evenly.
The dough will be very moist and sticky.

Shape biscuits by dropping blobs of dough into a bowl of flour.
Cover dough in flour so that it is easy to handle.
Shake excess flour from dough.
Press dough gently into a ball and place on a baking sheet.

Bake biscuits for 10-15 minutes at 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
