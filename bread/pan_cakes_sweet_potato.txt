
--------------------------------------------------
Pan Cakes with Pumpkin or Sweet Potato
--------------------------------------------------

----------
Ingredients

- flour: 1 cup
- baking powder: 1 tablespoon
- salt: 1 teaspoon
- cinnamon: 2 teaspoons
- milk: 0.33 cup
- egg: 1
- pumpkin or sweet potato, cooked: 350 grams
- butter: 3.5 tablespoons


----------
Preparation

- combine and mix all ingredients
- cook on a hot skillet
