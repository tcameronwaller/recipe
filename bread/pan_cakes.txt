Pan Cakes
date, review or revision: 3 January 2025
----------

----------
Ingredients

flour: 1 cup
bake powder: 1 tablespoon
sugar: 1 teaspoon
salt: 0.25 teaspoon

oil or butter: 2 tablespoons
eggs: 2
milk: 0.75 cup

options
- use half wheat flour and half cornmeal
- substitute water for eggs and milk
- adjust liquid (eggs and milk or water) for proper consistency
- add vanilla extract (1 teaspoon), cinnamon (1 tablespoon) or other spices and seasonings
- add frozen blueberries, raisins, or other fruits to the pancakes while cooking
